Our Donate Plan

The money from this fundraiser will be distributed to those that are already working (within that area) in this situation and the rest will go to food, water, medicine, and necessities for those affected people.

Food for a family

We will provide dry food, clean drinking water to every family affected by the floods Basic food staples for children and cash assistance to buy food, Hygiene kits: Sanitary napkins, hand soap, buckets etc. Animal feed etc.

Shelter for a family

We will take them to a safe haven. We will make arrangements so that they can stay safe until the situation improves. And we will help those whose houses are damaged to repair their houses.

Education for a child

We will make arrangements for the children who go to school to start reading again. I will provide books to those whose books have been destroyed and I will provide necessary financial assistance for the purchase of educational materials.
